<h1>GymFinder</h1>
GymFinder helps the fitness community to find out the best gym in their locality.

<h2>Features</h2>
<h3>For Users</h3>
<li>
    <ul>Register, locate, and review your local gyms</ul>
    <ul>Interact with other gym users and share your experiences</ul>
    <ul>Receive updates on the latest gym offers and discounts</ul>
</li>
<h3>For Gym Owners</h3>
<li>
    <ul>Register your gym and promote visibility to the community</ul>
    <ul>Interact with your gym members and get feedback on your services</ul>
</li>

<h2>Installation</h2>
<p>To install the project, you need to have Java 23 and Maven installed on your machine. 
You can download Java from [here](https://www.oracle.com/java/technologies/javase-jdk11-downloads.html) and Maven from [here](https://maven.apache.org/download.cgi).</p>
